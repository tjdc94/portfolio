Premier League Player xG vs xA 20/21

The visualisation below shows the relationship between Expected Goals (xG) & Expected Assists (xA) for each outfield player, that played over 1000 minutes, in the 2020/2021 EPL season. You can use the dropdown menu on the left to filter by position or by club. Hover over each point to display more informtion about the player

Premier League Player Pizza Chart 20/21

The visualisation below shows more in depth metrics for each outfield player in the Premier League that played more than 1000 minutes. You can use the drop down menu to select a position and then select the player name to display their chart. Each metric is measuered as a percentile that ranges from 1 to 99. A player scoring in the 99th percentile for a metric means that they scored higher than 99% of players in their position. Metrics are broken up into three categories, Attacking, Possession & Defensive metrics

1000 IMDB Movies by Genre

The visualisation below highlights the relationship between IMDB rating and the genre of the top 1000 rated movies on IMDB. The size of the bubbles denotes the movie's Gross Earnings. Hover over each bubble to display more information about the movie. The total number of movies in each genre is displayed below each boxplot. You can hide or unhide each category of movie by clicking on the legend.

World Happiness & GDP Maps

The two maps below shows the happiness score & the relative GDP score of each country in the world. A high happiness score denotes a happier country. The same logic applies to the GDP Score. You can hover over each country to see more information about the country. Click the map then scroll or use the + and - symbols in the top left corner to zoom in and out
